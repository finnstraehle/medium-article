How to Not Go Crazy While Coding

A simple 10-steps guide to prevent implosions

Step 1: Embrace the "Code-nutrition"

Just like your body needs balanced nutrition, so does your coding brain! Keep a healthy balance of code, coffee, and snacks. Remember, too much caffeine can lead to "code-ffee" crashes!

Step 2: Dance It Out

Every few hours, take a coding break and have a spontaneous dance party! Shake off those coding bugs and do the "JavaScript Jive" or the "Python Polka." It's impossible to go crazy when you're grooving!

Step 3: Master the Art of Talking to Your Rubber Duck

When you hit a coding roadblock, explain your code out loud to a rubber duck. It may sound silly, but "rubber duck debugging" works wonders. You'll either solve the issue or have a new coding buddy!

Step 6: Embrace the Zen of Tabs vs. Spaces

Don't get caught up in the tabs vs. spaces debate. Embrace the peaceful coexistence of both. Remember, inner peace is the key to sanity in coding!

Step 8: Surround Yourself with Plants

Step 9: Practice "Pair-owling":

Pair programming is great, but pair programming with an owl is even better! Find an owl plushie or use a picture of an owl as your pair programming buddy. Their wise presence will keep you grounded.

Step 10: Set a Timer for Social Media

It's easy to get lost in the social media black hole while coding. Set a timer for your social media breaks, or you might end up in the "Twitter-verse" forever!
